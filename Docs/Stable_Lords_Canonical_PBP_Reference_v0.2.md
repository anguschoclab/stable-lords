## PBP Structure

### 1. Pre-Bout Introduction Block
Each warrior gets 4-7 lines describing:
- Height (derived from SZ)
- Race/origin (flavor)
- Handedness: "is right handed" | "is left handed" | "fights as well with either hand"
- Armor description with adjectives ("fine, well-oiled suit of LEATHER armor" | "is checking the straps of his LEATHER armor" | "has drawn on a suit of SCALEMAIL armor")
- Helm: "and wears a FULL HELM" | "and will wear a STEEL CAP" | "and will wear a LEATHER CAP"
- Weapon with action verb ("grins down his SCIMITAR at his foe" | "is swinging a BROADSWORD" | "is drawing a LONGSWORD from its sheath")
- Off-hand weapon: "and carries a MEDIUM SHIELD for parrying" | "and flips a DAGGER in his off-hand" | "and carries a HATCHET in his off-hand"
- Fighting style declaration: "is a WALL OF STEEL fighter" | "will fight using the attack style of LUNGING" | "uses the TOTAL PARRY style"
- Weapon suitability statement: "is well suited to his weapon" | "is well suited to the weapons selected" | "does not seem to have the STRENGTH to use this weapon well" | "hasn't the DEFTNESS this weapon requires for best use"
- Backup weapon: "He has a back-up SCIMITAR at his side" | "He has a DAGGER thrust into his waistband"

### 2. Battle Opener
Single atmospheric line:
- "The battle begins amid the crowd's approval."
- "Those in the stands shift their attention to the warriors."
- "The audience falls silent as the dueling begins."
- "The time for the fight has come."
- "The warriors advance on each other."
- "The signal is given for the duel to begin."
- "The audience rises as the fighting begins."
- "The battle begins" (terse variant)

### 3. Combat Exchanges
Each exchange follows: Initiative → Attack → Defense → Counter → Hit → Damage → State

**Attack verbs** are weapon-type specific:
- Slash weapons: "slashes with his SCIMITAR!", "slashes an arcing attack", "leaps into the air taking a furious slash"
- Bash weapons: "bashes with his MAUL!", "smashes downward", "smashes with his MEDIUM SHIELD!"
- Thrust weapons: "lunges wielding an EPEE!", "stabs powerfully upward", "thrusts his LONGSWORD forward with an unbelievably deadly force!"
- Fists: "PUNCHES from the waist!", "FISTS punching with piston-like power!"

**Defense verbs** vary by type:
- Parry: "parrys with his SCIMITAR", "blocks the blow with his MAUL", "parries the attack using his MEDIUM SHIELD"
- Dodge: "contorts his body inhumanly as he unbelievably dodges the blow!", "successfully dodges his opponent's attack!", "ducks out of the path of the attack"
- Shield: "defense is secure as his MEDIUM SHIELD easily parrys the attack!"

**Counterstrike sequences**:
- "pivots around seeking the counterstrike!"
- "pivots around, seeking the counterstrike!"
- "steps back, and then rushes forward in a counterstrike!"
- "feints an attack, freezing his opponent's initiative!"
- "feints an attack, hindering his opponent's initiative!"
- "bats his foe's weapon aside leaving him open to attack!"
- "disengages his foe's weapon arm and tries to steal the initiative!"
- "allows his foe to over-extend himself"
- "ducks under the onrushing foe, looking for the counterstrike!"

### 4. Hit Location Specificity
Canonical DM uses very specific body part names:
- **HEAD**: JAW, TEMPLE, THROAT, SKULL, FOREHEAD, FACE, side of the HEAD
- **ARMS**: RIGHT FOREARM, LEFT BICEPS, RIGHT ELBOW, RIGHT HAND, RIGHT ARM, LEFT ARM
- **LEGS**: RIGHT THIGH, LEFT THIGH, RIGHT KNEE, LEFT KNEE, RIGHT SHIN, RIGHT BUTTOCKS, LEFT RIBCAGE, RIGHT RIBCAGE
- **TORSO**: CHEST, RIBS, STOMACH, PELVIS, KIDNEYS, GROIN, BELLY, ABDOMEN, NECK, upper BODY, LOWER BODY

## Key Narrative Patterns from FTF26 Archive

### 1. Armor Interaction
Armor frequently deflects or reduces damage:
- "The attack bounces off the armor." (explicit negation)
- "The stroke lands ineffectively." (armor implied)
- "has been wounded in the ABDOMEN!" followed by severity implies penetration

### 2. Desperation Mechanics
When HP drops critically low, warriors enter DESPERATION:
- State announcement: "Our Aruaki Werecat fights with DESPERATION!"
- Behavior: increased offense, reduced defense
- Visual: "eyes flame with DESPERATION!"
- Often precedes either comeback or death

### 3. Style-Specific Patterns
- **Wall of Steel (WS)**: Heavy armor, shield parries, endurance-focused, counterstrikes
- **Lunging (LU)**: Opening burst damage, "lunges forward", "springs viciously forward", thrust verbs
- **Striking (ST)**: Sustained offense, "slashes an arcing attack", "ducks low, SCIMITAR slicing suddenly upwards"
- **Total Parry (TP)**: Defensive stalling, "allows his foe to over-extend", energy conservation
- **Aimed Blow (AB)**: Precise targeting, "hacks viciously downward"

### 4. Initiative Control
Battle momentum visualized through initiative mechanics:
- "feints an attack, freezing his opponent's initiative!"
- "disengages his foe's weapon arm and tries to steal the initiative!"
- "bats his foe's weapon aside leaving him open to attack!"
- Successful parry → counterstrike opportunity

### 5. Weapon Drops & Recovery
Warriors can lose weapons mid-fight:
- "is visibly staggered and drops his SCIMITAR!"
- "pulls his SCIMITAR free and spins on his opponent" (recovery)
- Adds dramatic tension to close fights
